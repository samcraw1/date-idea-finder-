# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 12, 2026**

## Date Idea Planner

This Privacy Policy describes how Date Idea Planner ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our ChatGPT application.

## Information We Collect

When you use Date Idea Planner, we receive the following information:
- **City/Location**: The city you specify for date recommendations
- **Preferences**: Budget level, vibe preference, time of day, and relationship stage you select

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:
- Generate personalized date recommendations
- Fetch relevant venue data from Google Places API
- Provide outfit suggestions and conversation starters

## Data Storage

- We do **not** store your personal information
- We do **not** create user accounts or profiles
- Queries are processed in real-time and not retained
- No conversation history is saved on our servers

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Places API**: To fetch venue information. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by their service.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
